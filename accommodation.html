Accomodations
University Residence Hall

Students attending the conference are eligible to reserve a dorm room in a residence hall at the University of Pennsylvania. The rate for accommodation is $59 per bed, per night. Additionally, there is a one-time charge of $150 for a dorm essentials set, which is applicable for the entire duration of your stay. This set is designed to provide you with necessary items for your dorm room.

Student housing is only for a single registered conference attendees; no guests or family members can stay in the dorms.

Please note that while you will have a private room, the suite will be shared with other students.

After registering for the conference, you will receive a separate email containing a link for the dorm room reservation.

Sheraton University City

Conference Rate: $159 / room night + taxes

Address: 3549 Chestnut St, Philadelphia, PA 19104 (8 minutes walking distance to Houston Hall)

If you intend to stay at this hotel, you can make reservations using this link: Book Your Room

You can also make reservations/arrangements directly with Marriott reservations at 1-888-627-7071 or (215) 387-8000. Please mention that you are a participant of the IC2S2 Conference 2024. Participants are responsible for settling their own lodging costs directly with the hotel. Please be advised that due to limited room availability, we highly recommend making your reservations as early as possible. If the rooms are sold out, you may consider the alternative accommodations listed below.
