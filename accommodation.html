Accomodations

University Residence Hall

Students attending the conference are eligible to reserve a dorm room in a residence hall at the University of Pennsylvania. The rate for accommodation is $59 per bed, per night. Additionally, there is a one-time charge of $150 for a dorm essentials set, which is applicable for the entire duration of your stay. This set is designed to provide you with necessary items for your dorm room.

Please note that while you will have a private room, the suite will be shared with other students.

After registering for the conference, you will receive a separate email containing a link for the dorm room reservation.

Sheraton University City

Conference Rate: $159 / room night + taxes

Address: 3549 Chestnut St, Philadelphia, PA 19104 (8 minutes walking distance to Houston Hall)

If you intend to stay at this hotel, you can make reservations using this link: Book Your Room

You can also make reservations/arrangements directly with Marriott reservations at 1-888-627-7071 or (215) 387-8000. Please mention that you are a participant of the IC2S2 Conference 2024. Participants are responsible for settling their own lodging costs directly with the hotel. Please be advised that due to limited room availability, we highly recommend making your reservations as early as possible. If the rooms are sold out, you may consider the alternative accommodations listed below.

Scandic Nørreport, Frederiksborggade 18, 1360 København K ★★★★

  • Distance from venue: 1.4 km
  • Distance from airport: 10 km
  • Nearest station: Nørreport station, S-trains and Metro, M1, M2 (0m)
  • Rates: 196€ single, 210€ double

A stylish and buzzing boutique hotel in central Copenhagen created for both travelers and locals. Scandic Nørreport is located less than a minute’s walk from Nørreport station in the city centre, amid cyclists, coffee-drinking locals and the town's beautiful old buildings. On the other side of the street, Torvehallerne offers cuisine from all over the world, and close by the hotel, you can visit the city's art museums, shop for vintage and designer clothes along the narrow cobblestone streets or explore Copenhagen's Michelin restaurants.

Hotel Kong Arthur, Nørre Søgade 11, 1370 København ★★★★

  • Distance from venue: 1.5 km
  • Distance from airport: 12.5 km
  • Nearest station: Forum Station, M1, M2 (850m)
  • Rates: 205€ single, 239€ double

Nestled in a small cobblestone nook, just off the street that runs along Copenhagen's historical lakes, you will find Hotel Kong Arthur. Once a home for young boys, but now a hotel for everyone. Hotel Kong Arthur still has the original outer appearance from 1882 – except for the hotel sign above the front door. Inside, the interior is modern, with classic Danish furniture and art, a fireplace, herringbone parquet floors, and high panels. The rooms may be very different, but one thing is certain: You'll find the same kind of peaceful atmosphere and warmth in all the unique rooms, in the spa, and by the fireplace in the lobby.

Wake-up, Borgergade 9, 1300 København K ★★

  • Distance from venue: 2.0 km
  • Distance from airport: 9 km
  • Nearest station: Kongens Nytorv station, M1, M2, M3 (500m)
  • Rates: 115€ single

Wakeup Copenhagen in Borgergade offers modern and good-value accommodation in central Copenhagen, only 5 minutes walk from Kongens Nytorv, Nyhavn and the shopping street, Strøget. All 498 rooms have air-conditioning, flat-screen TV, a small work desk and bathroom with shower. Please note that all rooms have one 160 cm wide double bed (no twin beds) and are non-smoking. The building is designed by Danish architect Kim Utzon and has 6 floors. Facilities include a breakfast restaurant, business centre, and free WiFi.

Cabinn Scandinavia, Vodroffsvej 55, 1900 Frederiksberg ★★

  • Distance from venue: 2.5 km
  • Distance from airport: 12.5 km
  • Nearest station: Forum Station, M1, M2(400m)
  • Rates: 99€ single, 116€ double

4-minute walk from Forum metro station and 2 km from both Rosenborg Castle and Tivoli Gardens. The relaxed rooms have free Wi-Fi, flat-screen tv and work desks, as well as tea and coffee making facilities. Some have bunk beds. Amenities include a bright lobby lounge, a coffee shop and a guest computer. There are also meeting rooms. Breakfast buffet is available for a fee.
